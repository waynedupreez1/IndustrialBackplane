Issues with shields and hats

1. Messy wiring interconnects when having multiple hats and shields stacked.
2. Never enough 24V compliant Inputs/Outputs.
3. Not having a wide input voltage power supply cards for powering boards.
4. Unable to use the same hats and shields with other sbc's and microcontrollers available.

Some Ideas around a workable solution

1. Must be modular.
2. Must be expandable.
3. Must be useable with any microcontroller or single board computer.
